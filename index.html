üç∞ Chocolate Cake

Indulge in this heavenly chocolate cake that melts in your mouth! This rich, moist cake is layered with silky chocolate ganache and topped with decadent chocolate shavings. Perfect for celebrations or when you need a sweet pick-me-up!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot coffee
  • Chocolate ganache for frosting

üçØ Gulab Jamun

These golden, spongy balls of pure bliss are soaked in aromatic sugar syrup infused with cardamom and rose water. A beloved Indian sweet that's crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, and bursting with sweet, fragrant flavors that will transport you to dessert paradise!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 3-4 tablespoons milk
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1 cup sugar (for syrup)
  • 1 cup water (for syrup)
  • 4-5 green cardamom pods
  • Few drops rose water
  • Chopped pistachios for garnish

üç¶ Vanilla Ice Cream

Cool down with this classic vanilla ice cream that's smooth, creamy, and absolutely irresistible! Made with real vanilla beans and fresh cream, this frozen delight is perfect on its own or as a companion to your favorite desserts. Each spoonful delivers pure, cold happiness!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 vanilla bean (scraped)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: chocolate chips or nuts

ü•õ Kalakhand

This soft, milk-based Indian sweet is like a cloud of sweetness that dissolves on your tongue! Made from fresh paneer and condensed milk, Kalakhand has a unique grainy texture and delicate sweetness that's enhanced with cardamom and garnished with pistachios. Simply divine!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1/4 cup chopped pistachios
  • 1/4 cup chopped almonds
  • Silver leaf for decoration (optional)
